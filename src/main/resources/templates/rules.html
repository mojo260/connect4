Connect 4 Rules

Connect 4 is a two-player connection game in which the objective is to be the first to get four of your pieces in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Basic Gameplay

  1. The game is played on a grid of 7 columns and 6 rows.
  2. Each player takes turns dropping a piece of their color (either red or yellow) into one of the columns.
  3. A piece will fall to the lowest available slot in the chosen column.
  4. The goal is to connect four of your pieces in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Winning the Game

    The first player to connect four of their pieces in a row wins the game. The pieces can be connected in the following ways:

  • Horizontally: Four pieces in a horizontal line across the grid.
  • Vertically: Four pieces stacked in a vertical line.
  • Diagonally: Four pieces arranged diagonally in any direction.

Game End

    If the grid is filled and no player has connected four pieces, the game is a draw.

Important Notes

  • Players cannot move a piece once it has been dropped into a column.
  • Players should alternate turns and cannot skip their turn.
